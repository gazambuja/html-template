Câu hỏi thường gặp

01. Which types of website are accepted ?

We accept almost all types of website! But your website must comply with our terms.

02. Which ad formats are supported and how many ad units can be placed on a single page?

- We support standard ad formats: 300x250, 728x90, 468x60, 120x600, 160x600, 800x440, Popup, Pop-under.

- You can only put up to 3 ad tags and Pop-under on a single page.

03. How can I increase my earnings ?

Revenue is based on many factors such as Clicks, Country and website content. The best way to increase revenue is moving advertise closer to your website content.

04. How do you check Fraud or SPAM ?

We do not tolerate any kind of Fraud or SPAM and we take stern action to check it. We will terminate the account immediately and all the earnings of the Publisher will be forfeited.

05. Can I put ad tags on various websites ?

No, Adonly Ad tags only appear on the approved site.

06. When will I get paid ?

You can request payment whenever you reach the minimum payment threshold $10. Payment will be taken 3 times a month, on the 10th, 20th, and 30th.
For example: You request payment on 3rd then you will get paid on the 10th, if you request payment on the 11th you will receive payment on the 20th.

07. What is the payment threshold and what are my payment methods ?

1. Our minimum payment threshold is $10.

2. Currently we only offer PayPal, Payza, Payoneer as payment methods.

3. For Payoneer, you need to reach threshold ($20) before we send you the payment.

08. What will I get if I introduce a new member ?

You will earn 5% of the income of publishers that you have introduced.
All your earnings from referrals will be summarized at the end of month. Then, you can request payment on the first day of the next month.

09. I do not get any clicks on an ad, what will I get ?

The ads are working but if you do not get any click, you will not receive revenue.

10. If my ads get more clicks, will I receive more revenue ?

If you receive many clicks on ads, surely, your revenue will be high.
